## Welcome to Alter

Alter is a macOS AI assistant that lives in your notch, providing seamless AI-powered assistance through voice commands, text input, and contextual awareness.

## Start in 10 Minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Alter and grant permissions">
    Go to the [Quickstart guide](/quickstart) and complete the install and setup steps.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Learn the core interaction modes">
    Open [Getting Started](/guides/getting-started) and try:
    - Hovering the notch
    - Opening with your shortcut
    - Speaking a simple request
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up reliable context and tools">
    Enable **Flow** and apply basic context habits from:
    - [Use Flow](/guides/use-flow-orchestration)
    - [Managing Context Window](/guides/managing-context-window)
  </Step>
</Steps>
